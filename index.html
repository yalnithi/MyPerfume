About Us

My Perfume is perfume bar that sells high-quality, value for money fragrances with unparalleled expertise in the art & science of perfumery. Having roots in Calicut, a coastal town in Kerala boasting a legacy of international trade and travel since the 1800s, our journey began amidst a crowd of perfume enthusiasts who adored traditional ouds and attars. Our early days were dedicated to thorough research and market testing to develop a blend suitable for the Indian climate. Through persistence, we curated a formula ensuring a delightful and enduring fragrance. MYOP's first product line achieved the impossible: high-quality, value for money perfumes.
